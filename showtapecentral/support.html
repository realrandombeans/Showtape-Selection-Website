Help & Support

Help for all things Showtape Central

General

Showtape Central is completely free to play!

Either you can hit the Download button on the home page, or search it up on Gamejolt.

To play Showtape Central, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • A computer running Windows 7 or above
  • An internet connection

Accounts

You may not be able to sign in for a variety of reasons. One of the main reasons is that you have forgotten your password. If you cannot remember your password, please launch up the game, press Reset Password, enter your email, and you should get an email to reset your password.

There can be many reasons why you may not get an email. First, make sure your email is correct, if it is not, you will have to create a new account. If you are sure it is correct, then it may be a problem on our end. If so, please contact us at contact@showtapeselection.com!

Your passwords aren't accessible by us, nor is any personal data except your email. If you have any concerns about privacy, don't be afraid to contact us!

For account deletion requests, email us or DM one of the developers in the Discord server.
